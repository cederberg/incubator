## General Guidelines
- Always use brief and concise wording.
- Confirm feature additions with user.

## Skill Writing
- Prefer open-ended instructions; agents are frequently competent to figure things out
- Codify rules when evidence indicates a problem or to shortcut required exploration
- Confirm changes with user first, then batch edits in one pass
- After edit, offer to spawn sub-agent reviewer to check brevity, clarity, usefulness, etc.
