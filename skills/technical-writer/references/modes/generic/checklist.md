# Structural Checklist: Generic

Verify each item explicitly.

- [ ] Summary opens with a factual statement of what the subject is; no taglines or marketing phrases
- [ ] Concepts & Definitions is present if any term is used in more than one section; absent otherwise
- [ ] No term is used before it is defined in Concepts & Definitions or introduced in its own section
- [ ] No section heading is a placeholder (e.g. `[Topic]` was replaced with a real name)
- [ ] No section is present that has no content relevant to the reader
- [ ] No `[MISSING: ...]` placeholders remain in the final draft
