# Structural Checklist: Generic

## Structure

- [ ] Summary section is present
- [ ] Section order: Summary → Concepts & Definitions (if present) → topic sections
- [ ] Summary opens with a factual statement of what the subject is; no taglines or marketing phrases
- [ ] Summary identifies the subject scope
- [ ] Summary identifies explicit exclusions from scope
- [ ] Concepts & Definitions is present if and only if a term is used in more than one section
- [ ] No section heading contains a placeholder such as `[Topic]` or `[Process Name]`
- [ ] No section is present whose content does not pertain to the document's stated subject

## Terminology

- [ ] No term is used before it is defined in Concepts & Definitions or introduced in its own section
